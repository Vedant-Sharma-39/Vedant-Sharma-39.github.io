Microbial Strategy Simulation

Explore how microbial populations adapt their resource utilization strategies (Glucose vs. Galactose) on a spatial grid with varying nutrient gradients. Orange cells prefer Glucose (high 'P' threshold), while blue cells switch more readily to Galactose (low 'P' threshold). Click on the grid to plant an initial population. Adjust parameters using the controls.

Simulation based on ecological and evolutionary principles.
