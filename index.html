Microbial Strategy Simulation Project

Exploring ecological and evolutionary dynamics using agent-based modeling.

About the Simulation

This project simulates the spatial spread and adaptation of microbial populations competing for resources (Glucose and Galactose) on a hexagonal grid. Cells evolve a strategy ('P' threshold) determining their preference for nutrients based on local availability. The simulation incorporates factors like fitness-dependent colonization success and competition for space.
